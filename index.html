expo gamification

event vendors used this app, created on Eventfinity using React and Firebase, to interact with one another at their expo shows. interactions with other booths earned them points to place them on a leaderboard, which was on display throughout the event. they could also check their progress via a custom "event passport". over 2000 attendees participated in this experience!

nominated for a BizBash Event Experience Award: Best Event Technology

lights ON

this event experience sought to encourage attendee engagement by turning their corporate logo into a custom light-up wall, created using React and Firebase. users were able to select one of the 257 lightbulbs on screen and light up the wall from their phones, and they were able to slowly watch the wall get brighter as more people joined in. with a final participation measurement of 95%, this app got some real "glowing" reviews from our clients!

nominated for an Experience Design & Technology Award: Best High-Tech Audience Interaction
